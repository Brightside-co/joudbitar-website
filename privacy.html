Privacy Policy

How Joud Bitar collects, uses, and protects your personal information.

Last updated: March 2026

1. Who We Are

This privacy policy applies to joudbitar.co, operated by Joud Bitar. For any privacy-related enquiries, contact: info@joudbitar.co

2. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly when you:

  • Submit an enquiry through the contact form
  • Complete an intake questionnaire
  • Register for a program through a JotForm link
  • Communicate with us via email or WhatsApp

This may include: name, email address, phone number, health background, and dietary information provided voluntarily for the purpose of receiving services.

3. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used exclusively to:

  • Deliver the programs or services you have enrolled in
  • Respond to enquiries and provide support
  • Send program-related communications
  • Personalise guidance based on health information you share

We do not use your information for advertising or sell it to third parties.

4. Health Information

Health and nutrition information shared during intake or coaching is treated with strict confidentiality. It is used solely to provide tailored guidance and is never shared with third parties without your explicit consent.

5. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party tools to operate our services:

  • JotForm — for intake forms and program registration
  • Formspree — for contact form submissions
  • WhatsApp — for group program communication
  • Zoom — for live sessions
  • YouTube — for video content delivery

Each of these services has its own privacy policy. We encourage you to review them.

6. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as is necessary to deliver services and maintain records. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us at info@joudbitar.co.

7. Cookies

joudbitar.co is a static website and does not currently use tracking cookies. If this changes, this policy will be updated accordingly.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent for communications at any time

To exercise any of these rights, email: info@joudbitar.co

9. Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your personal information. However, no method of data transmission is completely secure and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. The updated version will always be available at joudbitar.co/privacy.html with the date of the latest revision.

Questions? Contact: info@joudbitar.co
